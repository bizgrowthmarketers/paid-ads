  • Our Process

    • Step 1: Discovery & Consultation

      We begin with an in-depth consultation to understand your business, goals, and target audience. This helps us craft a strategy that’s perfectly aligned with your objectives.

    • Step 2: Strategy Development

      Based on our findings, we develop a custom ad strategy that includes everything from keyword research to audience targeting and ad creative.

    • Step 3: Campaign Setup

      We handle all aspects of campaign setup, including account creation, ad group structuring, keyword selection, and creative development.

    • Step 4: Ongoing Management & Optimization

      Our team continuously monitors your campaigns, making data-driven adjustments to improve performance and maximize ROI. This includes bid management, A/B testing, and audience refinement.

    • Step 5: Reporting & Analysis

      We provide regular reports that break down campaign performance, giving you clear insights into your ROI and actionable recommendations for further optimization.
